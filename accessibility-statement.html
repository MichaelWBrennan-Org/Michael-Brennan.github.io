Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to digital accessibility and inclusive design for all users.

Last Updated: October 2025

Michael Brennan ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to ensure our website is accessible to all users.

WCAG 2.1 AA Compliant
Conformance Level: We strive to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

Our Commitment

We believe that digital accessibility is a fundamental right and essential for creating an inclusive online environment. Our commitment includes:

  • Ensuring our website is accessible to users with disabilities
  • Following WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines and best practices
  • Regular accessibility testing and improvements
  • Providing alternative access methods when needed
  • Training our team on accessibility principles

Assistive Technologies

Our website is designed to work with various assistive technologies, including:

  • Screen Readers: NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver, TalkBack
  • Voice Control Software: Dragon NaturallySpeaking, Voice Control
  • Magnification Software: ZoomText, MAGic, built-in browser zoom
  • Switch Navigation: Switch Access, Switch Control
  • Eye Tracking: Tobii Eye Tracker, Eye Gaze

Keyboard Shortcuts

We have implemented keyboard shortcuts to improve navigation efficiency:

  • Tab: Move to next interactive element
  • Shift + Tab: Move to previous interactive element
  • Enter/Space: Activate buttons and links
  • Escape: Close modals and menus
  • Arrow Keys: Navigate within menus and lists

Browser Compatibility

Our website is tested and optimized for the following browsers and assistive technologies:

  • Chrome (latest version) with NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver
  • Firefox (latest version) with NVDA and JAWS
  • Safari (latest version) with VoiceOver
  • Edge (latest version) with NVDA and JAWS
  • Mobile browsers with built-in accessibility features

Testing and Evaluation

We use multiple methods to test and evaluate our website's accessibility:

  • Automated Testing: We use tools like WAVE, axe, and Lighthouse to identify accessibility issues
  • Manual Testing: Our team conducts manual testing with various assistive technologies
  • User Testing: We work with users with disabilities to test our website
  • Expert Review: Accessibility experts review our website for compliance
  • Regular Audits: We conduct quarterly accessibility audits
