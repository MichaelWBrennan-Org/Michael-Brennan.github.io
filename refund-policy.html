Refund Policy

Terms and conditions for refunds and cancellations of our services and products.

Last Updated: October 2025

This Refund Policy ("Policy") describes the terms and conditions under which Michael Brennan ("we," "our," or "us") provides refunds for our services and products. By purchasing our services or products, you agree to this Refund Policy.

💰 Refund Overview

We offer refunds for most services within 30 days of purchase, subject to the terms outlined below. Refund eligibility depends on the type of service and completion status.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available for the following services under the specified conditions:

💻 Web Development Services

Refundable within 7 days of project start if no significant work has been completed. Partial refunds available based on work completed.

🎮 Game Development

Refundable within 14 days of purchase for downloadable games. No refunds for completed custom game development projects.

🤖 AI Automation Services

Refundable within 30 days if the service does not meet agreed specifications. Full refund if service is not delivered.

📱 Mobile App Development

Refundable within 14 days of project start if no significant work has been completed. Partial refunds based on development progress.

🔧 Consulting Services

Refundable within 48 hours of service delivery if not satisfied. No refunds for completed consulting sessions.

📚 Digital Products

Refundable within 7 days of purchase for digital downloads. No refunds for consumed or downloaded content.

Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

1. Contact Us

Email us at michaelwilliambrennan@gmail.com with your refund request. Include your order number and reason for the refund.

2. Review Process

We will review your request within 2-3 business days and determine if you are eligible for a refund based on our policy.

3. Refund Processing

If approved, we will process your refund within 5-10 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

4. Confirmation

You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been processed and the funds have been returned to your account.

Non-Refundable Items

The following items and services are not eligible for refunds:

  • Completed Projects: Fully completed web development, mobile app, or game development projects
  • Custom Work: Custom software, scripts, or solutions that have been delivered and accepted
  • Consulting Sessions: Completed consulting sessions or advisory services
  • Third-Party Services: Services provided by third-party vendors or partners
  • Digital Downloads: Software, games, or digital content that has been downloaded or accessed
  • Subscription Services: Ongoing subscription fees for services already provided
  • Domain Names: Domain name registrations or renewals
  • Hosting Services: Web hosting or server services that have been used

Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds based on the following criteria:

  • Work Completed: Refund amount will be reduced by the value of work already completed
  • Materials Used: Costs for materials, licenses, or third-party services already purchased
  • Time Invested: Compensation for time and effort already invested in the project
  • Administrative Fees: Small administrative fee may be deducted for processing

⚠️ Important Note

Partial refunds are calculated based on the percentage of work completed and may not reflect the full amount paid. We will provide a detailed breakdown of the refund calculation.

Refund Timeframes

Refund processing times vary depending on the payment method and service type:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 7-14 business days
  • Cryptocurrency: 1-3 business days
  • Check Refunds: 10-14 business days

Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with our refund decision, you may:

  1. Request Review: Ask for a second review of your refund request with additional documentation
  2. Escalate: Request to speak with a senior team member about your case
  3. Alternative Resolution: We may offer alternative solutions such as service credits or additional work
  4. Third-Party Mediation: In extreme cases, we may agree to third-party mediation

Chargebacks and Disputes

If you initiate a chargeback or dispute with your payment provider:

  • We will provide all necessary documentation to support our case
  • We may suspend your account until the dispute is resolved
  • We reserve the right to refuse future services if chargebacks are found to be fraudulent
  • We encourage you to contact us directly before initiating a chargeback

Service Credits

In some cases, we may offer service credits instead of monetary refunds:

  • Future Services: Credits can be applied to future services or projects
  • Extended Support: Additional support or maintenance services
  • Upgrades: Service upgrades or additional features
  • Expiration: Service credits typically expire within 12 months of issuance

✅ Satisfaction Guarantee

We stand behind our work and are committed to your satisfaction. If you're not happy with our services, we'll work with you to find a solution that meets your needs.

Special Circumstances

We may make exceptions to our refund policy in the following circumstances:

  • Technical Issues: If our service fails due to technical problems on our end
  • Misrepresentation: If we significantly misrepresented our services or capabilities
  • Force Majeure: If circumstances beyond our control prevent service delivery
  • Medical Emergencies: In cases of serious medical emergencies affecting the client
  • Natural Disasters: In cases of natural disasters or other catastrophic events

Refund Request Requirements

To process your refund request, we need the following information:

  • Order Information: Order number, date of purchase, and service details
  • Contact Information: Full name, email address, and phone number
  • Reason for Refund: Detailed explanation of why you're requesting a refund
  • Supporting Documentation: Any relevant screenshots, emails, or other evidence
  • Payment Information: Original payment method and transaction details

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Refund Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy or need to request a refund, please contact us:

  • Email: michaelwilliambrennan@gmail.com
  • Subject Line: Refund Request - [Your Order Number]
  • Website: michaelwbrennan.github.io
