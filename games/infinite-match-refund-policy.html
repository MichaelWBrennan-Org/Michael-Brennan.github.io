Refund Policy

Infinite Match - Match-3 Puzzle Game

Last Updated: October 2025

This Refund Policy explains the refund terms and conditions for Infinite Match, a Match-3 puzzle game developed by Michael Brennan ("we," "our," or "us"). Please read this policy carefully before making any in-app purchases.

Quick Summary

Refunds for in-app purchases are generally handled by your app store (Apple App Store, Google Play Store, etc.) and are subject to their respective refund policies. We may provide refunds in exceptional circumstances at our discretion.

General Refund Policy

All in-app purchases for Infinite Match are processed through third-party app stores (Apple App Store, Google Play Store, etc.). Refund requests must be submitted through the appropriate app store platform.

App Store Refund Policies

  • Apple App Store: Refunds are handled through Apple's refund system. Contact Apple Support or visit Apple's refund page
  • Google Play Store: Refunds are handled through Google Play. Visit Google Play refund page
  • Other Platforms: Contact the respective platform's support team

Refund Eligibility

Refund eligibility depends on several factors:

Purchase Type Refund Eligibility Time Limit Conditions
Eligible Accidental Purchase Yes 48 hours Must not have used the item
Eligible Technical Issues Yes 7 days Must be verified by our support team
Eligible Duplicate Purchase Yes 24 hours Must be verified through purchase history
Conditional Change of Mind Maybe 24 hours At our discretion, must not have used item
Not Eligible Used Items No N/A Items that have been consumed or used
Not Eligible Game Progress No N/A Virtual currency or items already spent

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Contact App Store

First, try to request a refund through your app store:

  • iOS: Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Media & Purchases > View Account > Purchase History
  • Android: Open Google Play Store > Menu > Account > Purchase History
  • Other Platforms: Check the platform's support documentation

Step 2: Contact Our Support

If the app store cannot process your refund, contact our support team:

  • Email: michaelwilliambrennan@gmail.com
  • Subject Line: "Refund Request - Infinite Match"
  • Include: Purchase receipt, reason for refund, and any relevant details

Important Information

We cannot process refunds directly. All refunds must go through the app store where you made the purchase. We can only assist with technical issues or provide guidance on the refund process.

Refund Processing

If we approve a refund request, here's what happens:

Processing Time

  • App Store Refunds: 3-5 business days
  • Direct Refunds: 5-10 business days
  • Bank Processing: Additional 3-5 business days

Refund Method

  • App Store Credits: Refunded to your app store account
  • Original Payment: Refunded to your original payment method
  • Bank Account: May take additional time to appear

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are generally not eligible for refunds:

  • Consumed Items: Power-ups, boosters, or currency that has been used
  • Game Progress: Levels completed, achievements unlocked, or scores earned
  • Time-Based Items: Items that expire or have limited duration
  • Account Bans: Refunds due to account suspension or termination
  • Fraudulent Activity: Purchases made through fraudulent means

Special Circumstances

We may consider refunds in the following special circumstances:

Technical Issues

  • Game crashes preventing item usage
  • Items not appearing in game after purchase
  • Duplicate charges for the same item
  • Server issues preventing item delivery

Documentation Required

  • Purchase receipt or confirmation email
  • Screenshots of the issue
  • Device information and game version
  • Detailed description of the problem

Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with our refund decision, you may:

  • Appeal: Submit additional documentation or evidence
  • Escalate: Contact our support team for further review
  • Third-Party: Contact your app store's dispute resolution team
  • Legal: Pursue legal remedies as permitted by law

Our Commitment

We are committed to fair and transparent refund practices. We will review each case individually and make decisions based on the specific circumstances and our refund policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the game
  • Sending notifications through the game interface
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Email notification for significant changes (if you've provided contact information)

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy or need assistance with a refund request, please contact us:

  • Email: michaelwilliambrennan@gmail.com
  • Website: michaelwbrennan-org.github.io
  • Game Support: Available through the game's help section
  • Response Time: We will respond to refund inquiries within 48 hours
  • Business Hours: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM EST
