Privacy Policy

Infinite Match - Match-3 Puzzle Game

Last Updated: October 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Michael Brennan ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you play Infinite Match, a Match-3 puzzle game built with Unity and comprehensive backend services.

Quick Summary

We collect minimal data necessary for game functionality, progress saving, and analytics. We never sell your data and use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Information We Collect

Game Data

We collect the following information to provide game services:

  • Game Progress: Level completion, scores, achievements, and save states
  • Gameplay Analytics: Time spent playing, features used, and performance metrics
  • In-App Purchases: Purchase history and transaction data (processed by app stores)
  • Cloud Save Data: Game progress synchronized across your devices

Device Information

We automatically collect certain device information:

  • Device Type: Mobile, tablet, or desktop device
  • Operating System: iOS, Android, Windows, macOS, or Linux version
  • Hardware Specs: CPU, RAM, and graphics capabilities for optimization
  • Network Information: Connection type and quality for cloud save functionality

Unity Analytics Data

Our game uses Unity Analytics to improve gameplay experience:

  • Performance Metrics: Frame rate, loading times, and crash reports
  • User Behavior: How players interact with game features
  • Technical Data: System specifications and compatibility information
  • Error Logs: Crash reports and error messages for debugging

Important Note

We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers unless you voluntarily provide them through support requests or feedback forms.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Purpose Data Used Legal Basis
Game Functionality Game progress, save data Contract performance
Cloud Save Services Game progress, device info Contract performance
Performance Optimization Analytics, device specs Legitimate interest
Bug Fixes & Updates Crash reports, error logs Legitimate interest
Customer Support Contact info, game data Contract performance

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Unity Technologies: For analytics and crash reporting services
  • Cloud Storage Providers: For secure data storage and synchronization
  • App Store Platforms: For in-app purchase processing (Apple, Google, etc.)
  • Analytics Services: For game performance and usage analytics

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent fraud or security threats
  • Enforce our terms of service

Data Protection Promise

All third-party service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the specific services they provide to us.

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure Servers: Data stored on secure, monitored servers
  • Access Controls: Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing

Operational Safeguards

  • Staff Training: Regular privacy and security training
  • Data Minimization: Collect only necessary information
  • Retention Limits: Delete data when no longer needed
  • Incident Response: Procedures for handling security breaches

Data Retention

We retain your information for different periods depending on the type of data:

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Game Progress Until account deletion Service functionality
Analytics Data 2 years Performance improvement
Crash Reports 1 year Bug fixing
Support Communications 3 years Customer service
Purchase Records 7 years Legal compliance

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Portability

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate information

Control and Deletion

  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Object: Opt out of certain data processing

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at michaelwilliambrennan@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Infinite Match is designed to be suitable for all ages, but we take children's privacy seriously:

Age Restrictions

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If you are under 13, please do not provide any personal information
  • Parents should supervise their children's use of the game
  • In-app purchases may require parental consent

Parental Controls

Parents can:

  • Monitor their child's gameplay and purchases
  • Contact us to request deletion of their child's data
  • Disable in-app purchases through device settings
  • Use parental control software to manage game access

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws:

  • Adequacy Decisions: Transfers to countries with adequate protection
  • Standard Contractual Clauses: EU-approved data transfer agreements
  • Privacy Shield: US companies with Privacy Shield certification
  • Consent: Your explicit consent for transfers

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the game
  • Sending notifications through the game interface
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Email notification for significant changes (if you've provided contact information)

Your Continued Use

Your continued use of Infinite Match after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
