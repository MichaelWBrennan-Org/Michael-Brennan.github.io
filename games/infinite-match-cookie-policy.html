Cookie Policy

Infinite Match - Match-3 Puzzle Game

Last Updated: October 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Michael Brennan ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you play Infinite Match, a Match-3 puzzle game built with Unity and comprehensive backend services.

Quick Summary

We use minimal cookies necessary for game functionality, progress saving, and performance analytics. You can control cookie settings through your device or browser preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website or use an app. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and enabling certain functionality.

Types of Cookies We Use

Infinite Match uses the following types of cookies:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Essential
Essential Game Session Maintain game state and progress Session only Yes
Essential Save Data Store game progress and settings Persistent Yes
Analytics Performance Monitor game performance and crashes 1 year No
Functional Preferences Remember game settings and preferences 6 months No
Analytics Usage Understand how players use the game 2 years No

How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the game to function properly and cannot be disabled:

  • Game Session Cookies: Keep track of your current game session
  • Save Data Cookies: Store your game progress and achievements
  • Security Cookies: Protect against fraud and ensure secure connections
  • Cloud Save Cookies: Enable progress synchronization across devices

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how players interact with our game:

  • Performance Monitoring: Track game performance and identify issues
  • Usage Analytics: Understand which features are most popular
  • Crash Reporting: Collect error information to fix bugs
  • Device Information: Optimize game for different devices

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your gaming experience:

  • Preference Storage: Remember your game settings
  • Language Selection: Remember your preferred language
  • Display Settings: Remember your graphics and audio preferences
  • Accessibility Options: Remember accessibility settings

Third-Party Cookies

Our game may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

Unity Technologies

Unity Analytics and Unity Services may set cookies for:

  • Game performance analytics
  • Crash reporting and debugging
  • User behavior analysis
  • Technical diagnostics

Privacy Policy: Unity Privacy Policy

App Store Platforms

When making in-app purchases, app stores may set cookies for:

  • Purchase verification
  • Payment processing
  • Transaction security
  • Receipt validation

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies:

Device Settings

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Privacy > Ads
  • Windows: Settings > Privacy > General
  • macOS: System Preferences > Privacy & Security

Browser Settings

If playing through a web browser, you can control cookies through browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

Important Note

Disabling essential cookies may prevent the game from functioning properly. Some features, such as cloud save, may not work if you disable all cookies.

Cookie Consent

When you first launch Infinite Match, you may see a cookie consent notice. This allows you to:

  • Accept All: Allow all cookies for the best experience
  • Essential Only: Only allow cookies necessary for game functionality
  • Customize: Choose which types of cookies to allow
  • Learn More: Access detailed information about our cookie usage

Withdrawing Consent

You can withdraw your consent at any time by:

  • Changing your device privacy settings
  • Clearing your browser data
  • Uninstalling and reinstalling the game
  • Contacting us directly

Data Retention

We retain cookie data for different periods depending on the type:

Cookie Type Retention Period Reason
Session Cookies Until you close the game Maintain game state
Save Data Cookies Until you delete save data Preserve game progress
Analytics Cookies Up to 2 years Performance improvement
Preference Cookies 6 months Remember settings

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding cookies:

Access and Information

  • Right to Know: Information about what cookies we use
  • Right to Access: Details about cookies stored on your device
  • Right to Portability: Export your cookie preferences

Control and Deletion

  • Right to Delete: Remove cookies from your device
  • Right to Restrict: Limit certain types of cookies
  • Right to Object: Opt out of non-essential cookies

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the game
  • Showing a notification when you launch the game
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Email notification for significant changes (if you've provided contact information)
